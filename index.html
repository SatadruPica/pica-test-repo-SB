About the Agency

The Armed Detective Agency operates as a supernatural detective organization based in Yokohama. Our team consists of individuals with extraordinary abilities who dedicate their lives to protecting the city and its inhabitants.

Founded on the principles of justice and literary appreciation, we handle cases that ordinary law enforcement cannot resolve. Each member brings unique skills and abilities to our investigations.

We work closely with local authorities while maintaining our independence, ensuring that justice prevails in even the most challenging circumstances.
